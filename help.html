Help & Support

How to Post a Notice

To post a new notice, go to the Notices section from the sidebar and click on "Add Notice". Fill in the title, content, and department. Once done, click Submit.

Managing Staff

To manage staff, navigate to the Staff section. You can add new staff members, update roles, or remove existing records using the buttons available.

Changing Your Password

Go to the Settings page and enter your new password in the password field. Make sure to click Save Settings after making any changes.

Troubleshooting

If you experience issues like slow loading, failed submissions, or login problems, ensure you have a stable internet connection and try refreshing the page. If the issue persists, contact support.
